How it works

What is Widgetizr?

A lightweight widget engine for Linux. No Electron bloat, no subscriptions — just beautiful, useful apps living on your desktop.

🌐

Any URL as a Widget

Drop any website or local web app onto your desktop as a floating, always-on-top widget. If it runs in a browser, it runs in Widgetizr.

💾

Your Data, Your Files

Notes and todos sync directly to plain files on your filesystem. Open them in Obsidian, vim, or anything else.

🔒

No Cloud, No Lock-in

Everything runs locally on your machine. Widget data stays local, though Widgetizr may fetch version information to notify about updates. Yours, forever.

Install

Get started in 30 seconds

One command to install Widgetizr. Requires GJS and WebKitGTK on Linux.

Terminal
